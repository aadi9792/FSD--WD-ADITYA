ABESEC

ABES Engineering College (ABES EC), Ghaziabad is one of the leading technical institutions in Uttar Pradesh, affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow. Established in 2000, ABES is known for its academic excellence, strong industry connections, modern infrastructure, and emphasis on innovation and research.

The college offers undergraduate (B.Tech) and postgraduate (M.Tech, MBA, MCA) programs in various disciplines of engineering, management, and computer applications. With highly qualified faculty, state-of-the-art laboratories, smart classrooms, and a vibrant campus life, ABES provides students with holistic growth opportunities.

ABOUT

ABES is consistently ranked among the top private engineering colleges in Uttar Pradesh and NCR, with a strong record in placements, internships, and industry collaborations. Its mission is to nurture skilled professionals and future leaders who contribute positively to society.
